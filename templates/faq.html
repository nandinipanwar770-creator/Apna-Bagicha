Organic Certification

PGS (Participatory Guarantee System) India Government ki organic certification hai jisme farmers ek dusre ke practices verify karte hain. Ye NPOP certification se chhote farmers ke liye accessible alternative hai. PGS certified products par premium price milti hai aur export ke liye bhi valid hai.
Haan, hamare PGS-India certified organic products mein koi synthetic chemical, pesticide residue, ya GMO material nahi hota. Vermicompost, neem cake, karanja cake, aur bio-pesticides sab natural sources se banaye jaate hain aur third-party lab testing se confirm hote hain.
PGS-India ke according minimum 2 saal ka conversion period hota hai. Pehle saal mein normally yield thodi kam hoti hai, lekin soil health improve hone par 2-3 saal mein yield aur quality dono badhti hain. Hamare agronomist aapko step-by-step conversion plan banane mein free help karenge.
