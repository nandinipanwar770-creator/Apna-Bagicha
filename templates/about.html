Our Journey

From an Idea to a Growing Green Startup

We are just getting started — here's how Apna Bagicha came to life.

2023

The Spark

The idea was born — to build an accessible online platform where home gardeners and small farmers could find quality organic seeds, plants, and tools all in one place.

💡
🚀
Early 2024

We Launched

Apna Bagicha went live with our first catalogue of garden plants, seeds, grow bags, and tools. Our first customers placed orders and shared their feedback — and we loved every moment.

Mid 2024

First Milestones

Crossed our first 100 happy customers. Added more product categories, improved delivery, and launched our free agronomist helpline to support buyers with expert guidance.

🌿
📦
2025

Growing Fast

Expanded pan-India delivery, introduced online ordering and payment, and built a growing community of home gardeners and farmers who trust Apna Bagicha.

Next

What's Ahead

Mobile app, subscription boxes, farmer training programs, and partnerships with organic certification bodies — the best of Apna Bagicha is still to come. 🌱
